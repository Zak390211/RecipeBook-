Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti300g
Ground beef400g

Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Meanwhile, sauté onion and garlic in olive oil, then add the beef. Cook until browned. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer. Combine with pasta and serve hot with grated cheese.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour2 1/4 cups
Chocolate chips2 cups

Cream the butter and sugars until fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla, then mix in the dry ingredients. Fold in chocolate chips. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes. Let cool before serving.

Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Mixed vegetables3 cups
Soy sauce3 tbsp

Heat oil in a wok. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5–7 minutes. Add soy sauce and stir to coat evenly. Cook 2 more minutes. Serve over steamed rice or noodles for a quick, healthy meal.

Source: Love & Lemons
